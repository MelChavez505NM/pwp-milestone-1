This project will be a professional website that will focus on the front-end web development for the purpose of an tax-accounting, audit, and book-keeping services, with a strong focus on Oil and Gas accounting.

Audience

My website is designed to be user-friendly for all individuals, so that the audience can find the accounting services that they are seeking. The audience will come with different technical backgrounds from those who do not have any technical background to professionals that are technically savvy so that my accounting service is scalable. However, the target audience will be for business.

Goal

The goal of my website is advertise accounting and tax services. Second, is to tell the user a story about education and experience in Oil and Gas audit/accounting. Third, is to briefly educate the user regarding tax and accounting methodologies, and finally, to engage the user to contact me through intake form, contact us, or tax planner.
